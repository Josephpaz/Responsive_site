Who We Are?

We are a philanthropic academic initiative made up of senior developers and designers.

What's Our Goal?

Encourage the creation of projects, whether websites or apps. For you or your team who don't know where to start can reach the heavens. Splitting the process into creation, management, analysis. Succeeding in all three your project will surely be a success.

Create

The beginning of everything. First look for the problem, do it, brainstorm all the ideas, solutions to this problem. It's important to discuss it with others, to make your ideas on how to approach it more critical.

Manangement

Project management is undoubtedly one of the most important parts of the project, and you or your team must start building the process of how to solve the problem by making flowcharts or case diagrams. Drafting processes, functions that will be done will make it easier as you go through project creation.

Analyze

Finally, the analysis, defined the problem and the way it will be solved is time to start the reports, each defined step should be observed and done, this part of the project is important because if something goes wrong, difficulties are observed this is where will be exposed and discuss solutions to address the problem.

by Gustavo Fadel, Joseph Paz, Leonardo Viana
